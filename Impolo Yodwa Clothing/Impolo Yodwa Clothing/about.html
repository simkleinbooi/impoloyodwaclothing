Founder of ImpoloYodwaClothing

APHIWE SHOSHA

Impolo Yodwa is a local clothing brand that was established in 2016 by a young Designer and Entrepreneur, Aphiwe Shosha. He was born in Cape Town but raised in the village of Ngqamakhwe where he did his lower grades at Ntseshe J.S.S. and then moved back to Cape Town to continue with his high school academics at Manzomthombo High School, and he is currently studying Fashion Design

“Impolo Yodwa started off as just a logo of a man sitting in a chilled position that pupils used to draw on almost everything from, desks, chairs, hallway walls, toilet walls, etc, at a high school that I once visited and it stuck with me so I then asked a friend of mine to draw it on my school bag and I added the title Impolo Yodwa and made it his thing” said Aphiwe.

In 2016 he saw a gap in the fashion market and decided to start his own streetwear brand using social events as his selling channel. He started his business using other designers to see his garments but experienced some incompetencies as time went by. Aphiwe says, sometimes he would not deliver some orders on time because his supplier was either taking too long to produce his orders or sometimes he would not get ahold of him at all and that put a strain on his business and he would lose some of his turned customers and some potential ones.
